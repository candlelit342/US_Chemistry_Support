Ionic Charges Drop!

An ion formula falls from the top of the board. Use ← → to slide it into the correct lane, and ↓ to drop it faster. It locks in place only when it reaches its own correct box — so if you're in the wrong lane, just keep watching and try the next one!

Match all 35 ions before you run out of lives to complete the chart. Correct placements score +10 with a "ping"; a miss costs −50 points and a life, with a very different buzz.
