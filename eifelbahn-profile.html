Distances are along the railway line — footpaths between stations will usually be somewhat longer. Walking times use ~4 km/h plus 1 min per 10 m of ascent (Naismith's rule). Elevations, km points and coordinates are approximate; the GPS marker snaps to straight lines between stations, so mid-leg readings can be off by 1–2 km in winding valley sections.
