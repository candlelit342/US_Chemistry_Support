Random Element Drop! Expert Edition

Built for students who already know the whole periodic table: instead of working through Period 1, Period 2, Period 3 and so on in order, all 118 elements — every period, the Lanthanide Series and the Actinide Series — are shuffled together into one pile and fall one at a time in completely random order.

Use the arrow keys to steer each falling block sideways and land it in its correct box before it reaches the bottom of its row. The block's landing row is fixed automatically by which element it is — the only guess you have to make is the column. Correct placements score more points the faster you find them; wrong placements cost points and send the block back up for another try.

Use the switches above the board any time — even mid-game — to build your own challenge: hide the category colours for double points, hide the ghost-letter hints for triple points, or tick the combined switch for a flat four times the points. For the ultimate challenge, tick "Remove all scaffolding! The expert level!": every box on the entire table is hidden completely (not even an outline) until you solve it — a true blank sheet, with boxes only appearing as you place them correctly. This is worth a flat six times the points, and stacks with the other switches.
