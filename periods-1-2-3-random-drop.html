Periods 1-3: The Expert's Version

This is the shuffled, expert take on the Periods 1-3 game. Instead of working through Period 1, then Period 2, then Period 3 in order, all 18 elements from H through Ar are shuffled together into one pile and fall one at a time in completely random order.

Use the arrow keys to steer each falling block sideways and land it in its correct box before it reaches the bottom of its row. The block's landing row is fixed automatically by which element it is — the only guess you have to make is the column. Correct placements score more points the faster you find them; wrong placements cost points and send the block back up for another try.

Use the switches above the board any time — even mid-game — to build your own challenge: hide the category colours for double points, hide the ghost-letter hints for triple points, or tick the combined switch for a flat four times the points. For the ultimate challenge, tick "Remove all scaffolding! The expert level!": every box on the Periods 1-3 board is hidden completely (not even an outline) until you solve it, and the decorative Periods 4-7 shown below disappear entirely too — a true blank sheet, with boxes only appearing as you place them correctly. This is worth a flat six times the points, and stacks with the other switches.
